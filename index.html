About Kick Pulse Studio

Kick Pulse Studio is an indie team focused on creating fast, instinct-driven gameplay. Our flagship title, Soccer Penalty Game, is built around precision, timing, and the rush you feel in a real penalty shootout.

We design our games so every action matters — every swipe, tap, shot, and save delivers that unmistakable pulse of excitement. Our mission is simple: make mobile sports games feel alive the moment you touch them.

Our Game

Soccer Penalty Game

Feel the tension, timing, and thrill of real penalty shootouts. Soccer Penalty Game is built for instinct-driven, high-energy mobile play — quick matches, tight controls, and pure gameplay.
