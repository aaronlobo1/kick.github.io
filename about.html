About Us

Our Story

Kick Pulse Studio is a indie team focused on creating fast, instinct-driven gameplay. Our flagship title, Soccer Penalty Game, is built around precision, timing, and the rush you feel in a real penalty shootout.

We design our games so every action matters — every swipe, tap, shot, and save delivers that unmistakable pulse of excitement. Our mission is simple: make mobile sports games feel alive the moment you touch them.

Kick Pulse Studio began with one question: “What if penalty kicks actually felt exciting on a phone?” We saw a gap in mobile sports games — missing tension, timing, and the thrill of instinct.

We experimented with dynamic timers, moving targets, goalkeeper reaction mechanics, camera animations, dive physics, and more to bring penalty shootouts to life.

What we Believe

  • Gameplay first — graphics follow the fun.
  • Mobile games should feel smooth, not complicated.
  • Every tap, swipe, and dive should excite the player.
  • If a mechanic doesn’t feel right, we rebuild it.
  • Games should be fun before anything else.
